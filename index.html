About

16OC does real feet-inch-fraction math plus right-angle & rafter solutions, stairs, area & volume, roofing, materials and trig.

It's a free Progressive Web App with no signup, no ads, and no subscription. Tap your browser's Share, then Add to Home Screen on iOS, or Install app on Android, to get an icon that launches full-screen and works offline.

The screen stays awake while the app is open, so it won't sleep between measurements.
