About

ConCalc is a construction calculator that does real feet-inch-fraction math, right-angle & rafter solutions, stairs, area & volume, roofing, materials, trig and more.

It's a Progressive Web App: tap your browser's Share → Add to Home Screen (iOS) or Install app (Android/Chrome) to get an icon that launches full-screen and works offline.
